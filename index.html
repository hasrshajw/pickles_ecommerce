Description

Crafted with authentic Andhra spices and slow-cooked mutton pieces, this traditional pickle brings the rich, bold flavours of homestyle cooking straight to your table. Each batch is prepared using age-old recipes passed down through generations, ensuring every bite delivers a burst of tangy, spicy goodness that pairs perfectly with rice, rotis, or parathas.
